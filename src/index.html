IV Rank Strategy

Sell puts when IV is historically elevated
Strategy Description

Sell puts when IV is high relative to its own 52-week history. IV Rank = (current IV − min IV) / (max IV − min IV) × 100. IV Percentile = % of days in the past year where IV was below today's level.

IVR ≥ 70 — Strong signal (+2 pts)
IVR ≥ 50 — Moderate signal (+2 pts)
IVR < 50 — Weak / no signal

IVR builds accuracy over time as the app accumulates scan history. Expect "—" until at least 10 scans are recorded for a stock.

IV vs HV Strategy

Sell puts when the market overprices future volatility
Strategy Description

Sell puts when the market is overpricing future volatility. Compare 30-day ATM implied volatility (IV) vs 30-day realized historical volatility (HV). A ratio IV/HV ≥ 1.3 means the market is paying more than the stock has actually moved — statistically favoring the options seller.

IV/HV ≥ 1.5 — Premium is significantly overpriced (+2 pts)
IV/HV ≥ 1.3 — Moderate volatility premium (+2 pts)
IV/HV < 1.3 — Insufficient premium

HV is computed fresh on each scan from the past 30 days of daily closes. IV/HV updates every time you run a scan.

Watchlist:
#SymbolScore PriceStrikeDTE IV%IV/HVIVR Ann. Yield

Mean Reversion Strategy

Sell puts after a fear spike, once IV starts declining
Strategy Description

Sell puts 1–2 days after a sharp IV spike, once IV confirms it is declining from the peak. Fear events (competitor earnings, macro data) cause IV to spike and then revert to the mean. Entry on day 2 of the decline captures high premium while reducing downside risk.

IV dropped ≥ 15% from recent peak (IVR was > 70) — Signal active (+1 pt)
No recent spike detected — Signal inactive

This signal requires at least 2 prior scans for a stock. The detector looks for a peak ≥ 40% IV in recent history with current IV at least 15% below that peak.

OptMax Guide

What is OptMax?

OptMax scans your watchlists for cash-secured put opportunities. You manage which stocks to screen for each strategy, and OptMax evaluates them against volatility signals to surface the best yield opportunities.

The 3 Strategies

IVR
IV Rank — Sell puts when IV is high relative to its own 52-week range. IV Rank = (current IV − min IV) / (max IV − min IV) × 100. Scores +2 points when IVR ≥ 50. Best used on stocks with consistent IV history.
IV/HV
IV vs HV — Sell puts when implied volatility exceeds 30-day realized (historical) volatility by ≥ 1.3×. This means the market is pricing in more movement than the stock has actually made, statistically favoring the seller. Scores +2 points when IV/HV ≥ 1.3.
MR
Mean Reversion — Sell puts 1–2 days after a fear spike, once IV is confirmed declining. OptMax detects a spike when IV in recent scan history reached ≥ 40% and current IV has since dropped ≥ 15% from that peak. Scores +1 point when signal is active.

Signal Score (0–5)

Each stock receives a composite score based on active signals:

  • ●● +2 pts — IVR ≥ 50
  • ●● +2 pts — IV/HV ≥ 1.3
  • ● +1 pt — Mean reversion signal active

A score of 4–5 indicates multiple signals aligning — the highest-conviction setups.

Common Filters (all strategies)

  • Open interest > 500 contracts
  • Bid-ask spread ≤ $0.10
  • Implied volatility > 15%
  • No earnings events within the option's expiration window

Stocks that don't pass these filters won't appear in scan results even if they're in a watchlist.

Managing Watchlists

Each strategy has its own watchlist. Type a ticker symbol and click + Add to add it. Symbols are validated against Yahoo Finance before being saved. Click × on a chip to remove a stock.

A stock can appear in multiple strategy watchlists and will show in each view with the appropriate badges.

Refresh Schedule

List Refresh — Runs a full options scan on all watchlist stocks and recomputes all metrics including HV, IVR, and IV/HV. Set your preferred interval (7–30 days) in the sidebar.

Price Update — Lightweight update that refreshes only the current stock prices. IV and volatility metrics are not recalculated. Set to Manual if you prefer to control timing.

IVR accuracy improves with each scan — the app stores per-symbol IV history and builds a 52-week range over time.
