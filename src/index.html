OptMax Guide

What is OptMax?

OptMax scans your watchlist for cash-secured put opportunities and scores each one on a unified 100-point scale across five dimensions: volatility, yield, strike quality, liquidity, and trend.

The 100-Point Score

Each stock is evaluated across five dimensions:

  • 35 pts — Volatility (IV Rank + IV/HV ratio)
  • 25 pts — Yield (monthly yield % + absolute IV)
  • 20 pts — Strike quality (delta + at-support)
  • 12 pts — Liquidity (open interest + bid-ask spread)
  • 8 pts — Trend (above MA50 + earnings clear)

Grades

  • A > 50 pts — Strong buy
  • B ≥ 40 pts — Good entry
  • C ≥ 30 pts — Marginal
  • D ≥ 20 pts — Weak
  • E ≥ 1 pt — Avoid
  • F Blocked — Score of 0 or Kill switch active

Kill Switches

Three conditions override the score to 0 (grade F) regardless of other signals:

  • Earnings event inside the option's expiry window
  • Bid-ask spread wider than $0.50
  • Absolute IV above 80% (danger zone)

Kill switches can be toggled individually in Settings → Scoring Configuration.

Screener & Filters

The Screener shows your opportunities with multi-column sorting (click any header to toggle ascending/descending order) and features interactive sliders to filter by Minimum Score, Min Monthly Yield, Min Market Cap, and a dual-thumb Price Range selector.

Watchlists & Favorites

Add tickers via the Screener input box. When a stock is watchlisted, the system immediately runs three automatic sync tasks (chart history, options scan, and price update) to fully populate it.

Click the Star column (☆/★) inside the Screener table or the details modal to mark a stock as a favorite. Starred favorites are tracked collectively in the main Starred Stocks view and on your Dashboard.

Refresh Schedule

Opportunity Data — Full options scan on all watchlist stocks. Run when you want fresh opportunity data. Also triggered automatically after a Discover scan.

Price Update — Lightweight update of current stock prices only. IV and volatility are not recalculated.
